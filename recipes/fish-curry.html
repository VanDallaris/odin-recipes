Smokey chorizo salmon

With coconut milk, korma curry paste & lime pickle

Ingredients

  • 500g ripe mixed-colour cherry tomatoes
  • 500 g white fish fillets
  • 1 heaped tablespoon korma curry paste
  • 1 table spoon lime/mango pickle
  • 1 x 400ml tin of light coconut milk
  • 4 sprigs of fresh basil

Steps

  1. Place 1 tablespoon of olive oil on a large shallow casserole pan on high heat
  2. Halve the tomatoes, add them to the pan skin side down and blister for 2 mins
  3. Chop the fish fillet into 4cm chunks
  4. Stir the fish fillet,korma paste, lime pickle and coconut milk. Bring to boil them simmer for 6 minutes
  5. Taste, season to perfection with salt and black pepper, then dish up wih basil leaves and enjoy your meal
