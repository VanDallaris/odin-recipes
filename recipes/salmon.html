Smokey chorizo salmon

With sweet cherry tomatoes & basil

Ingredients

  • 2 x 150 g salmon fillets
  • 300 g ripe mixed-colour cherry tomatoes
  • 4 sprigs of fresh basil
  • 8 black olives , (stone in)
  • 30 g chorizo

Steps

  1. Season your salmon with salt and pepper
  2. Put a table spoon of oil in a non-sticky pan and cook the salmon for about 8 mins
  3. Meanwhile, halve he cherry, tear the basil, and toss it all with 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar and a picnh of salt and black paper
  4. Squash the olives, chop them and mix with olive oil and water
  5. Finely chop the chorizo and add them for the last two minutes and toss the cherry tomatoes for 30 seconds
  6. Put the salmon on top of a plate, spoon over he dressed olives and add the remaining olives
